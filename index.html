Scott Whitmer

Mr. Whitmer has performed hundreds of evaluations and testimony in civil venues that cover personal injury, workers compensation, employment law, social security, catastrophic injury and death cases, and marriage dissolution cases to name a few.

Scott is the current President of the American Board of Vocational Experts from April, 2017 to April, 2019.

ABVE is a nationally recognized forensic organization with membership of approximately 500 members as well as a credentialing body for Forensic Experts who testify in many court venues and qualified to provide psychometric assessment and evaluation.

His professional credentials include:

  • Forensic Vocational Evaluator/Forensic Vocational Expert (FVE)
  • American Board of Vocational Experts/Diplomate (ABVE/D)
  • Vocational Rehabilitation Counselor (VRC)
  • International Psychometric Evaluation Certified (IPEC)
  • Licensed Mental Health Counselor (LMHC)
  • Nationally Certified Rehabilitation Counselor (CRC)
  • Nationally Certified Disability Management Specialist (CDMS)

Our Trusted Methodology

Every evaluation is put through our rigorous and methodological process.

Objective & Clinical

Vocational Evaluation has objective (empirically based) and clinical (expert qualitative peer reviewed) characteristics throughout its process.

Scientifically Based

Sound methodology relies upon and is undergirded with scientifically based constructs such as reliability, validity, triangulation and incremental validity across quantitative and qualitative protocols.

Productive Outcomes for Examinee

Sound evidence-based assessment and evaluation are meant to have efficacious outcomes for the examinee being evaluated as well as the court system for which the case is evaluated.
